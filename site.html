About me

My name is Mykola Kutsenko. I am 15 years old, and I currently study at a lycée in France. I have a deep passion for photography and am particularly interested in capturing landscapes and portraits. My journey with photography began recently, just two months ago, when I received my first camera as a gift. Since then, I've been exploring different techniques and styles, and I enjoy the creativity and storytelling that photography allows.

In addition to photography, I am also studying web design, focusing on HTML and CSS. The world of web design fascinates me because it combines both technical skills and creativity. I love the process of turning ideas into visually appealing and functional websites, and I'm constantly learning new techniques to improve my designs.

Music is another significant part of my life. I play the piano and enjoy performing pieces by classical composers like Beethoven and Chopin, as well as modern songs. Playing the piano is a way for me to express myself and unwind after a long day.

I've also had the opportunity to engage in various physical activities. I practiced karate, which taught me discipline and self-defense skills, and I also took dance classes, where I learned different styles and movements, enhancing my coordination and rhythm.

In my free time, I love watching TV series and movies. One of my favorite series is "Game of Thrones," which I enjoy for its intricate plot and complex characters. I am also a fan of science fiction, fantasy, and drama genres.

Living in France has been an incredible experience. I moved here with my family a few years ago, and adapting to a new culture and language has been both challenging and rewarding. I enjoy the rich history, beautiful

landscapes, and the diverse cultural experiences that France offers.

Overall, my interests and activities reflect my diverse passions and curiosity about the world. Whether it's through capturing a perfect photograph, designing a sleek website, playing a beautiful melody, or exploring new hobbies, I am always eager to learn and grow.
